🌱 Our Mission

ALIGN promotes inclusive digital collaboration by improving accessibility, affordability, and equal participation. We aim to reduce barriers by supporting equal access to digital tools, encouraging multilingual platforms, and ensuring everyone can participate in the digital world.

⚠️ Why Digital Inequality Matters

Digital inequality creates gaps in education, healthcare, and economic opportunities worldwide. It disproportionately affects low-income households, rural communities, and marginalized groups, deepening social and economic inequality — directly linked to SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities.

🎯 Who It's For

Students, educators, local communities, and anyone facing barriers to digital access — particularly those in rural areas, low-income households, and underrepresented groups.

✅ 3 Actions You Can Take

  • Advocate for affordable internet in your community
  • Support digital literacy programs for underserved groups
  • Create or share multilingual digital resources

🌟 3 Outcomes If We Act Together

  • Equal educational opportunities for all students
  • Economic empowerment through digital inclusion
  • Stronger, more connected communities
